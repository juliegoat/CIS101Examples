This assignment is designed to give us an understanding of each of the HTML elements. This is important so that we can put them together, and create a webpage. Paragraphs are used to break up blocks of text. It can sometimes be difficult to read large blocks of text on a website, and you may lose your viewer's attention.

You'll see that each paragraph will be separate from the last, even though they are right next to each other in the code. This makes the text on websites more readable. We tend to use paragraphs wherever we have text on our websites that is not a header.
